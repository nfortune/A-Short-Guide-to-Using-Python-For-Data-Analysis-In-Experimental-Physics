The offset represents any 'dc' offset in the photomultiplier amplifier (resulting in a nonzero signal in the absence of light), any additional light reaching the photodetector that didn't pass through the polarizers, and if the polarizers are not perfect (ideal), then this offset term will also include the fraction of  light passing through but not polarized by the polarizers.
