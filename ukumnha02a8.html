To do so, you precede the name of the function with np.  This is why the loadtxt command in this method is written np.loadtxt instead of loadtxt.  The use of the  np.prefix helps identify when the numpy package is being used but can become a nuisance when writing your own code. 
The choice of methods is yours, as long as you are consistent in your usage!
